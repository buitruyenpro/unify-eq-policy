## Policy v4 — split age eligibility

The legal pages now use a two-level age policy:

- **13+ core app:** on-device equalizer, local player, presets, profiles, effects, timers and editor tools.
- **16+ Community Presets:** Community access, account creation, profiles, publishing, downloading, comments, follows, likes, favorites, reposts, reports and all other Community interactions.

Updated files:
