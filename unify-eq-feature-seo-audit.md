# UNIFY EQ feature and SEO audit — v3

Source reviewed: `strings(1).xml` supplied on July 16, 2026.

## Main gap found in v2
The previous homepage covered the core equalizer engine well, but it did not expose the new Community product layer. The strings file contains a substantial Community system: preset and segment-config discovery, upload and download, search and filtering, creator profiles, follows/friends, online status, likes, favorites, comments, reposts, notifications, reports, blocking and moderation.

## Other important features now surfaced
- Local music library: songs, albums, artists, genres, playlists and favorites.
- Lyrics, metadata and album-art editing.
- Music Editor Pro with per-segment EQ preset switching.
- Smart queue modes: append, shuffle, skip duplicates, duration filtering and replace queue.
- A ↔ B repeat, playback speed, timestamp notes and seek shortcuts.
- Sleep timer, wake timer and recurring scheduled playback.
- Speaker/headphone profiles, preset albums, favorites, pin/duplicate/rename and JSON backup/restore.
- 100+ languages, audio knowledge guidance, themes and visualizers.
- Core engine: 16/31-band EQ, Crystal Clear DSP, smart bass, AGC mastering, compressor, limiter, noise gate, dynamics curves, reverb and 3D motion.

## Search intent covered
Primary: Android equalizer, equalizer app, bass booster, volume booster, 31-band equalizer.
Secondary: 3D/8D audio, AI mastering, music equalizer, local music player, community presets, share equalizer presets, Music Editor Pro.

## Important product distinction
Original music files remain local. Community use can transmit profile data, preset parameters, segment configurations and social interactions that the user intentionally publishes. The Privacy Policy, Terms and deletion page were updated to make that distinction explicit.
