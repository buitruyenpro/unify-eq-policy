01Introduction

Welcome to UNIFY EQ (the "App"), an audio equalizer and sound enhancement utility operated by an independent developer based in Vietnam. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store information when you use the App.

UNIFY EQ is a fully offline, local-only audio tool. The App does not require an account, does not use any sign-in provider, and does not include any social, community, or content-sharing features. All equalizer presets and settings are stored only on your own device.

13+

No accounts. No cloud. No community. UNIFY EQ does not ask you to sign in with Google or Facebook, does not create a user profile, and does not include preset-sharing, comments, or any social feature. Everything the App does happens locally on your device.

02Permissions & On-Device Data

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO / CAPTURE_AUDIO_OUTPUT): used only to analyze audio waves for the visualizer and equalizer processing on-device. We do NOT record, store, or transmit your audio.
  • Storage: saves your personal sound presets and exported share-card images locally on your device only.
  • Notifications: used only for local, on-device alerts directly related to the equalizer service (e.g. effect running in background). We do NOT send promotional, marketing, or remote push notifications of any kind.
  • System Alert Window, Foreground Service, Wake Lock, Exact Alarms: keep the audio effect service running across other apps.
  • Accessibility Service: declared with isAccessibilityTool=true. Used solely to detect audio session events from other media apps so the equalizer can adapt DSP processing in real-time. We do NOT read screen text, capture screen content, log keystrokes, or perform any action outside our equalizer DSP pipeline.
  • Billing: verifies Premium subscriptions purchased via Google Play.

03No Accounts, No Online Data Collection

UNIFY EQ does not require sign-in, does not offer Google or Facebook login, and does not create or store any user profile on a server. There is no community feature, no preset-sharing library, no creator profile, no comments, and no social interaction of any kind.

As a result, we do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Name, email address, or profile photo
  • Any account or user ID
  • Age verification records
  • Published presets, creator cards, comments, or feedback messages tied to an identity
  • Phone numbers, contacts, calendar, precise location, browsing history, financial info, or health data
  • Audio recordings

3.1. What stays on your device

  • Your custom equalizer presets and DSP settings
  • App preferences (e.g. theme, last-used preset)
  • Locally cached export images, if you choose to export a share card to your own gallery

None of this data leaves your device unless you manually export or share a file yourself using your device's standard share function. We have no server-side copy of it.

3.2. Limited technical data for billing & anti-fraud

  • Anonymous device ID (Android ID / Advertising ID) used solely to prevent trial abuse (e.g. uninstall-reinstall to reset a free trial), as permitted under Google Play's Device or other IDs policy for fraud prevention. This identifier is never shared with third parties for advertising and is reset when you delete the App.
  • Premium status flag (true/false), verified via Google Play Billing, to unlock paid features.
  • App version and basic diagnostic info needed to keep the equalizer service running correctly. Diagnostic logs, if any, are anonymized and not tied to an identity.

This is the only technical data involved in running the App; it is not linked to a name, email, or any personal profile because none exists.
