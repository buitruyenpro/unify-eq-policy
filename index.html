01Introduction

Welcome to UNIFY EQ (the "App"), an audio equalizer and sound enhancement utility operated by an independent developer based in Vietnam. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the App.

UNIFY EQ is not a social network. It is an audio tool with an optional preset-sharing library where users can publish their custom equalizer settings and audio configurations for other audiophiles to discover and apply.

Privacy-first commitment. We do NOT send marketing or promotional emails. We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your data. The only emails you may receive from us are transactional (purchase receipts, security alerts, important policy changes). Nothing else, ever.

02Permissions & On-Device Data

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO / CAPTURE_AUDIO_OUTPUT): used only to analyze audio waves for the visualizer and equalizer processing on-device. We do NOT record, store, or transmit your audio.
  • Storage: saves personal sound presets, cached avatars, and exported share-card images on your device.
  • Notifications: used only for in-app notifications about likes, comments, follows, and admin actions related to your preset-sharing activity. No promotional or marketing notifications are ever sent.
  • System Alert Window, Foreground Service, Wake Lock, Exact Alarms: keep the audio effect service running across other apps.
  • Accessibility Service: declared with isAccessibilityTool=true. Used solely to detect audio session events from other media apps so the equalizer can adapt DSP processing in real-time. We do NOT read screen text, capture screen content, log keystrokes, or perform any action outside our equalizer DSP pipeline.
  • Billing: verifies Premium subscriptions purchased via Google Play.

  • No subscription — including Lifetime — carries an obligation on us to operate cloud / server infrastructure indefinitely.

13Children's Privacy

UNIFY EQ is not directed at children under 13. We require an account to use the App, and account creation requires age verification.

How we verify age

  • Google "Family Link" / child-account flag is checked automatically — if true, signup is blocked
  • Facebook age_range API is checked automatically (if signed in via Facebook)
  • For users where no automatic signal is available, a self-declared age confirmation is required at signup
  • Behavioral signals are reviewed by moderators
  • A parent/guardian report channel is available (Section 17)

Users under 13

Account creation is blocked for users we identify as being under 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently created an account for a child under 13, we will:

  • Suspend the account within 24 hours
  • Delete all associated personal data within 30 days
  • Add the email to a permanent suppression list to prevent re-registration

Parents who believe their child has provided data should contact us at namtruyenpro95@gmail.com with subject Child Account Report.

Enhanced protections for users 13–17

Users aged 13–17 receive stricter privacy defaults regardless of region:

  • Profile is private by default (not visible to other users)

This aligns with the UK Age Appropriate Design Code (Children's Code) and similar frameworks.

Regional minimum age

In jurisdictions where the minimum digital consent age is higher (e.g. 16 in parts of the EU under Article 8 GDPR), local law applies and we treat the higher threshold as the minimum for account creation.
