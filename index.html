UNIFY EQ — Equalizer, Bass Booster & Audio Enhancer

Last updated • May 2026 • Version 2.0

01Introduction

Welcome to UNIFY EQ (the "App"), an audio equalizer and sound enhancement utility. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the App.

UNIFY EQ is not a social network. It is an audio tool with an optional preset-sharing library where users can publish their custom equalizer settings and audio configurations for other audiophiles to discover and apply. By using UNIFY EQ, you agree to the practices described below.

02Permissions & On-Device Data

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO / CAPTURE_AUDIO_OUTPUT): used only to analyze audio waves for the visualizer and equalizer processing on-device. We do NOT record, store, or transmit your audio.
  • Storage: saves personal sound presets, cached avatars, and exported share-card images on your device.
  • Notifications: used for in-app notifications about likes, comments, follows, and admin actions.
  • System Alert Window, Foreground Service, Wake Lock, Exact Alarms: keep the audio effect service running across other apps.
  • Accessibility Service: detects audio events from other apps so the equalizer adapts in real-time. We do NOT read text, capture screens, or collect anything outside our equalizer logic.
  • Billing: verifies Premium subscriptions purchased via Google Play.

03Accounts & Online Data

Most of the App works without an account. If you choose to sign in (Google or Facebook OAuth), the following data is collected and stored on our servers (Firebase):

3.1. Account & profile data (required if you sign in)

  • Name and email address (from Google / Facebook profile)
  • Profile photo URL (from Google / Facebook)
  • A unique user ID
  • Country code (e.g. "VN") for displaying a flag
  • Sign-in provider, account creation time, last login time

3.2. Profile content (optional, you can leave blank)

  • Bio / short description
  • Username (display handle)
  • Attribution handles: Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, GitHub (optional username field, used so other audio enthusiasts can find you on the platform where you primarily share audio content — not used for in-app social interactions)

3.3. Preset-sharing content (optional, only if you publish presets)

  • Creator cards — a public preview tied to your published preset (creator name, avatar, audio tier) so other users can attribute the preset to you
  • Feedback messages on shared presets (short notes about how a preset sounds, suggested usage, etc.)
  • Saves & creator subscriptions — users can save presets they like and subscribe to creators whose audio profiles they enjoy
  • Activity notifications generated when others save or comment on your published presets

This functionality exists solely to help audio enthusiasts discover quality presets and configurations. It is not designed for general-purpose social messaging.

3.4. Technical & usage data

  • Anonymous device ID (Android ID) used to prevent trial abuse (e.g. uninstall-reinstall to reset trial)
  • Total listening time and basic engagement counts for in-app stats
  • App version, basic diagnostic info needed for the equalizer service

We do NOT collect: phone numbers, contacts, calendar, precise location, browsing history, financial info, health data, or audio recordings.

04How Data Is Used

  • App functionality — rendering your creator profile, published presets, feedback messages, save/subscribe actions, activity notifications
  • Account management — authenticating you across sessions and devices
  • Personalization — showing your name, avatar, country, audio tier badge, attribution handles
  • Analytics — aggregate engagement metrics
  • Fraud prevention — detecting trial abuse and spam through device ID + dedup keys

We do NOT sell your personal data. We do NOT use it for advertising or marketing.

05Data Sharing

Data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure (Realtime Database, Authentication), a processor on our behalf governed by Google's data processing terms.

Public profile fields (name, avatar, bio, attribution handles, published presets, feedback messages) are visible to other users of the App. Email address, billing details, and device ID are never public.

We do not share data with third parties beyond Firebase (Google) and the standard Google Play billing flow.

10Premium Plans — Scope & Limitations

Important — please read before purchasing any Premium tier.

Premium tiers (Monthly / Yearly / Lifetime) primarily unlock offline audio features on your device. Online preset-sharing features depend on server infrastructure and are provided on a best-effort basis — not guaranteed forever or indefinitely.

10.1. What every Premium tier INCLUDES

All three tiers (Monthly, Yearly, Lifetime) grant access to the same Premium feature set, differing only in billing duration:

  • All premium equalizer bands, DSP effects, visualizers
  • Custom presets and personalization
  • Bass booster, virtualizer, stereo enhancer
  • Ad-free experience
  • Premium tier badge on share cards (visible to community)
  • Increased limits for preset publishing (attribution handles, extra preset slots)

10.2. What "Premium" does NOT promise

Online preset-sharing features — the public preset library, creator cards, feedback messages, save/subscribe actions, activity notifications — require a backend server that we operate at our own cost. These services are not guaranteed for the entire duration of any subscription.

  • Preset-sharing features may be discontinued, paused, modified, or rate-limited at any time for any tier — including Lifetime — if hosting becomes unsustainable, if features are abused, or if we redesign them.
  • We will provide at least 30 days' notice (in-app or via the Play Store listing) before permanently shutting down a major preset-sharing feature.
  • If preset-sharing features are permanently shut down, this is not grounds for a refund — because the Premium tier's primary value is the offline equalizer + DSP feature set, which continues to work on your device.
  • No subscription — including Lifetime — carries an obligation on us to operate cloud / server infrastructure indefinitely. Server availability depends on hosting cost, user count, and ongoing operating capacity.

10.3. Plan-specific scope

Monthly

  • Recurring billing every month via Google Play.
  • Access to all Premium features only while the subscription is active.
  • Preset-sharing access available during active period, subject to section 10.2.

Yearly

  • Recurring billing every year via Google Play (better value than monthly).
  • Access to all Premium features only while the subscription is active.
  • Preset-sharing access available during active period, subject to section 10.2.

★ Lifetime

  • One-time payment. No recurring charges.
  • Access to all Premium offline features for as long as the App remains published and installable from Google Play.
  • "Lifetime" refers to the lifetime of the App, not eternity, and not the lifetime of the preset-sharing server.
  • If we stop publishing the App on Google Play (e.g. business closure), Lifetime users may lose access without refund. We will give as much advance notice as practicable.
  • Preset-sharing access for Lifetime users is subject to the same best-effort terms as Monthly/Yearly — see section 10.2.

10.4. Why we have to say this

Servers, databases, and cloud bandwidth cost money every month. A one-time Lifetime payment cannot fund cloud hosting forever. By choosing any Premium tier, you acknowledge that:

  • You are paying for the offline Premium app functionality during the relevant billing period (or for life of the app, in the case of Lifetime).
  • Preset-sharing features are a bonus that may evolve, change, or end.
  • This is industry-standard practice for apps with optional cloud / preset-sharing features.

If you are uncomfortable with this, choose the Monthly plan, which can be cancelled at any time.
