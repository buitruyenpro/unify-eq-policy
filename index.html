01Introduction

Welcome to UNIFY EQ (the "App"), an audio equalizer and sound enhancement utility operated by an independent developer based in Vietnam. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the App.

UNIFY EQ is not a social network. It is an audio tool with an optional preset-sharing library where users can publish their custom equalizer settings and audio configurations for other audiophiles to discover and apply.

Your agreement. When you first open the App, you are shown a welcome screen with a link to this Privacy Policy and our Terms. By tapping Continue, you agree to everything described here. You can review or change communication preferences anytime in Settings → Notifications.

02Permissions & On-Device Data

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO / CAPTURE_AUDIO_OUTPUT): used only to analyze audio waves for the visualizer and equalizer processing on-device. We do NOT record, store, or transmit your audio.
  • Storage: saves personal sound presets, cached avatars, and exported share-card images on your device.
  • Notifications: used for in-app notifications about likes, comments, follows, admin actions, and product communications from our team (new features, app launches, audio tips, special offers). You can disable any notification category in Settings → Notifications.
  • System Alert Window, Foreground Service, Wake Lock, Exact Alarms: keep the audio effect service running across other apps.
  • Accessibility Service: declared with isAccessibilityTool=true. Used solely to detect audio session events from other media apps so the equalizer can adapt DSP processing in real-time. We do NOT read screen text, capture screen content, log keystrokes, or perform any action outside our equalizer DSP pipeline. A justification video has been submitted to Google Play in compliance with the Accessibility API policy.
  • Billing: verifies Premium subscriptions purchased via Google Play.

How we determine your region

To apply the right legal regime to your account, we evaluate three independent signals at account creation and re-check periodically:

  • Country code from your Google or Facebook account
  • IP geolocation at sign-in time (country level only — we do not store IP addresses long-term)
  • Device locale from your phone's language & region settings

If any of these three signals indicates an EU / UK / EEA / Swiss country, you are placed in the EU regime by default. This is a fail-safe design — we err on the side of stricter protection. You can correct your region in Settings → Account → Region if our detection is wrong (e.g. you are travelling).

10Premium Plans — Scope & Limitations

Important — please read before purchasing any Premium tier.

Premium tiers (Monthly / Yearly / Lifetime) primarily unlock offline audio features on your device. Online preset-sharing features depend on server infrastructure and are provided on a best-effort basis — not guaranteed forever or indefinitely.

10.1. What every Premium tier INCLUDES

All three tiers (Monthly, Yearly, Lifetime) grant access to the same Premium feature set, differing only in billing duration:

  • All premium equalizer bands, DSP effects, visualizers
  • Custom presets and personalization
  • Bass booster, virtualizer, stereo enhancer
  • Ad-free experience
  • Premium tier badge on share cards (visible to community)
  • Increased limits for preset publishing (attribution handles, extra preset slots)

10.2. What "Premium" does NOT promise

Online preset-sharing features require a backend server we operate at our own cost. These services are not guaranteed for the entire duration of any subscription.

  • Preset-sharing features may be discontinued, paused, modified, or rate-limited at any time — including for Lifetime users.
  • We will provide at least 30 days' notice before permanently shutting down a major preset-sharing feature.
  • Such shutdowns are not grounds for a refund — Premium's primary value is the offline equalizer + DSP feature set, which continues to work on your device.
  • No subscription — including Lifetime — carries an obligation on us to operate cloud / server infrastructure indefinitely.

10.3. Plan-specific scope

Monthly

  • Recurring billing every month via Google Play.
  • Access to all Premium features only while the subscription is active.
  • Preset-sharing access available during active period, subject to section 10.2.

Yearly

  • Recurring billing every year via Google Play (better value than monthly).
  • Access to all Premium features only while the subscription is active.
  • Preset-sharing access available during active period, subject to section 10.2.

  • One-time payment. No recurring charges.
  • Access to all Premium offline features for as long as the App remains published and installable from Google Play.

  • If we stop publishing the App on Google Play, Lifetime users may lose access without refund. We will give as much advance notice as practicable.
  • Preset-sharing access for Lifetime users is subject to the same best-effort terms — see section 10.2.

10.4. Why we have to say this

Servers, databases, and cloud bandwidth cost money every month. A one-time Lifetime payment cannot fund cloud hosting forever. By choosing any Premium tier, you acknowledge that:

  • You are paying for the offline Premium app functionality during the relevant billing period (or for life of the app, in the case of Lifetime).
  • Preset-sharing features are a bonus that may evolve, change, or end.
  • This is industry-standard practice for apps with optional cloud / preset-sharing features.
