# UNIFY Music SEO and feature audit

## Primary search intent targeted
- offline music player for Android
- MP3 player and FLAC player
- Hi-Res music player
- lossless music player
- 31-band equalizer and parametric EQ
- bass booster and 3D / 8D audio
- lyrics, playlists, gapless and crossfade
- Google Drive, Dropbox, WebDAV and NAS music player

## Technical SEO delivered
- One descriptive H1 and a structured H2/H3 hierarchy.
- Unique title and meta description.
- Canonical URL, robots directives and social metadata.
- SoftwareApplication, Organization, WebSite and FAQPage JSON-LD.
- Google Play install URL and Android package metadata.
- Descriptive image filenames, dimensions, alt text, lazy loading and image sitemap.
- Mobile responsive layout, semantic landmarks and reduced-motion support.

## Accuracy notes
- Hi-Res maximum is explicitly qualified by hardware and Android audio-path support.
- UNIFY is described as a player for files the user owns, not a public streaming catalog.
- Personal cloud/server features are described as connected sources, not music-download services.

## v5 keyword and content expansion
- Google Drive music player for Android
- Dropbox music player for Android
- WebDAV music player
- SMB and NAS music player
- cloud music player with offline cache
- progressive download music player
- Android music player themes
- 3D 360, 10D, 7.1 surround and spatial audio player

The copy avoids presenting UNIFY as a public streaming service. Connected sources are consistently described as personal cloud folders and private servers.
