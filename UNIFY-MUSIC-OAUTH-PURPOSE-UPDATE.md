# UNIFY Music OAuth homepage purpose update

The homepage now states above the fold that UNIFY Hi-Res Music Player Pro is:

- An Android offline music player for audio files the user owns or controls
- A local phone and supported SD-card player
- An MP3, FLAC, ALAC, WAV, OGG and AAC player
- A library, lyrics, playlist, theme and advanced-EQ application
- Not a public streaming catalog or music-download service

A new visible `#purpose` section explains:

- The core user purpose
- Local/offline playback
- Sound-processing features
- Why Google Drive read-only access is requested
- What UNIFY can and cannot do in Google Drive
- That Google Drive access is optional
- That local playback works without signing in
- That authorization can be disconnected or revoked

OAuth branding should use the exact public app name:
`UNIFY Hi-Res Music Player Pro`

Recommended homepage:
`https://unifyplayer.online/unify-music-home-page.html`
