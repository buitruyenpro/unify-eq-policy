Data Deletion Instructions

If you have logged into UNIFY EQ using your Facebook account and would like to delete your data, please follow the instructions below:

Option 1: Request via Email

  1. Send an email to: namtruyenpro95@gmail.com
  2. Subject: Facebook Data Deletion Request
  3. Include your account details (such as your email or Facebook ID)

We will process your request and delete your data within 3–5 business days.

Option 2: Remove the App from Facebook

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings
  2. Select Apps and Websites
  3. Find UNIFY EQ
  4. Click Remove to delete the connection and associated data

If you need further assistance, please contact us at namtruyenpro95@gmail.com.
