02Permissions & On-Device Data

  • Media / Audio files (READ_MEDIA_AUDIO / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): used to scan and play the music stored on your device. Your songs are played on-device only — we never upload, copy, or transmit your music files anywhere.
  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO / CAPTURE_AUDIO_OUTPUT): used only to analyze audio waves for the visualizer and equalizer processing on-device. We do NOT record, store, or transmit your audio.
  • Storage: saves personal sound presets, playlists, cached album art / avatars, and exported share-card images on your device.
  • Notifications: used for the media playback notification (play / pause / skip controls on the lock screen and notification shade) and for in-app activity notifications (likes, comments, follows related to your preset-sharing activity). We do NOT send promotional or marketing push notifications — all marketing happens via email and only with your opt-in.
  • Foreground Service, Wake Lock, System Alert Window, Exact Alarms: keep music playing in the background and keep the audio-effect service running across other apps.
  • Accessibility Service: declared with isAccessibilityTool=true. Used solely to detect audio session events from other media apps so the equalizer can adapt DSP processing in real-time. We do NOT read screen text, capture screen content, log keystrokes, or perform any action outside our audio / equalizer DSP pipeline.
  • Billing: verifies Premium purchases made via Google Play.

13Age Requirement & Children's Privacy

16+

You must be 16 or older

UNIFY Music's online account features are strictly for users aged 16 and above. Younger users cannot create an account or use the cloud features of the App.

Why 16?

We set the minimum age at 16 to ensure full compliance with the strictest applicable privacy laws governing minors, including:

  • Vietnam Decree 13/2023 on Personal Data Protection (Article 20 — children under 16 receive heightened protection)
  • EU GDPR Article 8 (default digital consent age of 16)
  • U.S. COPPA (we exceed the 13-year minimum to provide greater protection)
  • UK Age Appropriate Design Code

By choosing a higher minimum than legally required in some regions, we eliminate the need for region-specific parental-consent flows and ensure consistent protection for all younger people worldwide.

How we verify age

We use multiple layered signals to enforce the minimum age requirement:

  • Google "Family Link" / child-account flag — checked automatically at sign-in; if flagged, signup is blocked immediately
  • Facebook age_range API — checked automatically (if signed in via Facebook); accounts with min < 16 are blocked
  • Self-declared age confirmation — required at signup when no automatic signal is available. Users must tick "I confirm I am 16 years old or older" before they can proceed with Google sign-in.
  • Automated pattern detection — usernames, bios, and profile data are scanned for indicators of underage users (e.g. explicit mentions of school grade, birth year suggesting under 16). Flagged accounts are reviewed and removed if confirmed.
  • Parent/guardian report channel — see "How parents / guardians can report" below

Users under 16

Account creation is blocked for users we identify as being under 16. If we discover that we have inadvertently created an account for someone under 16, we will:

  • Suspend the account within 24 hours
  • Delete all associated personal data within 30 days
  • Add the email to a permanent suppression list to prevent re-registration and to block any future contact (transactional or marketing)
  • Notify the user (or their parent/guardian, if known) that the account has been removed

Marketing emails are never sent to users under 16, even hypothetically — the opt-in dialog only appears after age confirmation, and any account discovered to be underage is suppressed before any marketing send.

How parents / guardians can report

If you believe a person under 16 has created an account on UNIFY Music, please email namtruyenpro95@gmail.com with subject Underage Account Report. Include the account name or email if known. We will investigate within 7 business days and take action within 30 days.

No exceptions, no parental-consent workaround

To keep our compliance simple and protect young people, we do not offer a parental-consent path that would allow users under 16 to use the online features. There is no way to bypass the age requirement for accounts. This is intentional — we believe a clear, bright-line rule is safer than a partial exception that could be abused.
