# UNIFY EQ Policy Age Audit

## Required age model

| Feature group | Minimum age |
|---|---:|
| Core local equalizer, player and on-device audio tools | 13+ |
| Community Presets and all Community participation | 16+ |

## Community actions explicitly restricted to 16+

- Entering the gated Community area
- Creating or using a Community account
- Creating a public profile
- Browsing or downloading gated Community presets
- Publishing presets or Music Editor Pro segment configurations
- Likes, favorites, follows and creator subscriptions
- Comments, reposts, reports and notifications
- Online/recently-active status and other Community interactions

## Pages updated

- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Account & Data Deletion

## Important implementation note

The app interface and backend rules should enforce the same age split described by the policy. A policy statement alone is not sufficient if users aged 13–15 can still create a Community account or enter the Community area.


## Premium and Community separation

Community Presets must not be represented as a permanently guaranteed component of Monthly, Yearly or Lifetime Premium. Purchase screens and promotional copy should focus Premium value on eligible on-device/offline functionality and clearly label Community as an additional server-supported feature.
